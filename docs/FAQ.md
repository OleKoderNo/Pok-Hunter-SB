# Frequently Asked Questions

## Where are collections stored?

Inside

PokemonCatchData

---

## Can I move Streamer.bot?

Yes.

Just copy the PokemonCatchData folder.

---

## Will viewers lose their Pokémon?

No.

Collections are saved automatically.

---

## Does changing my Twitch username delete my collection?

No.

Collections use your Twitch User ID.

---

## Can I reset everyone's collection?

Yes.

Delete

collections.json

---

## Can I change shiny odds later?

Yes.

Future catches use the new odds.

Existing catches stay exactly the same.

---

## Does this require an internet connection?

Only to connect Twitch.

The Pokémon database is stored locally.

---

## Can I add more Pokémon later?

Yes.

Replace pokemon.json with a newer version.

Existing collections remain.
